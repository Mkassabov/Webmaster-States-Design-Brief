ABOUT ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

ar·ti·fi·cial in·tel·li·gence

noun

The development of computers capable of mimicking human cognitive abilities such as problem-solving, visual perception, decision-making, speech recognition, and translation.

Goals of AI

The abilities and potential of artificial intelligence are limitless. We have so many ideas about future applications of AI, and scientists are working hard to reach goals such as allowing computers to be able to perform tasks like reasoning, knowledge, planning, learning, natural language processing, perception, and the ability to move and manipulate objects. Creating systems that understand, think, learn, and behave like humans will be revolutionary and further advance society.

Benefits and Risks of Artificial Intelligence

Discussions about AI have gained quite a bit of momentum in the past year, with several luminaries speaking publicly about the threat of AI to humanity. AI can encompass anything from self-driving cars to autonomous weapons, so the safety of AI research is being heavily debated.
